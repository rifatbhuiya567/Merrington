Bookings & Payments

We have a strict booking & viewing policy which can be done via the fishery phone or via our Facebook page (message).

We take full payment for bookings payable by bank transfer or Paypal.

Peg slots run from 11.30am till 11am on the day of departure & pegs are allocated by at watercraft at 8.30am each morning.

We are minimum 24hrs on both of our Lakes, with weekends being minimum 48hrs (Friday till Sunday) & only have single swims on both.

emergency / out of hours contact

For any anglers onsite & requesting emergency assistance please call one of the 2 numbers located on the white sign on the front of the office cabin.
