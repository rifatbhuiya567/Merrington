Merrington Fishery Lakes

Main Lake: 8 Acres - 15 Swims

At 8 acres in size & having 15 comfortable single swims the mainlake is our specimen water with a rough stock of 275 fish!

  • 90% of those are over 20lb, 47 x known 30's & 2 known 40's to 43lb 8oz.

  • Lake records stand at 43lb 8oz (Mirror) & 41lb 8oz (Common).

  • The mainlake definitely has its rewards but is far from an easy water.

This lake is definitely not for beginners as blanks happen regularly to the most seasoned of anglers.

The lake average is 25/26lb.

Fishing can be tricky at times however as they say effort equals reward.

We operate on a day ticket basis and booking is essential prior to fishing.

Exclusive lake bookings can be arranged, please contact us on the fishery phone number to discuss dates and prices.

Our list of prices are as seen below.
  • Mainlake - per 24hrs - £32.50
  • Weekends minimum 48hrs.
Our booking slots run from 11.30am - 11am on both lakes.

But Mainlake anglers can arrive earlier for the watercraft peg draw at 8.30am.

Gates open at 8am.

We are currently taking full payment to secure bookings.

Once you have rung or messaged us to confirm dates, we will send you the account number & sort code for the transfer or the email address for paypal payments. (Please do not transfer money without contacting fishery first to confirm dates are available). These payments are non-refundable unless the fish spawn or the lake freezes as then we will close the fishery and refund all payments made or book you in for a later date.
